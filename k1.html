Koenigsegg Jesko Absolut: The Pinnacle of Speed

The Koenigsegg Jesko Absolut, unveiled in 2020, represents the ultimate expression of Koenigsegg's pursuit of speed and performance. Named after Jesko von Koenigsegg, the founder's father, the Jesko Absolut is designed to be the fastest production car in the world. With a top speed target exceeding 330 mph, it showcases an engineering marvel with cutting-edge technology and aerodynamics.

Powered by a 5.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine producing up to 1,600 horsepower, the Jesko Absolut combines blistering speed with exceptional handling and stability. The car’s advanced aerodynamics include an active rear spoiler and extensive use of lightweight materials, ensuring both high speed and aerodynamic efficiency.

With its blend of extreme performance, innovative technology, and exclusive design, the Jesko Absolut stands as a testament to Koenigsegg’s relentless pursuit of automotive excellence and speed.
