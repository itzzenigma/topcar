Lamborghini Sián FKP 37: A Revolutionary Hybrid Hypercar

The Lamborghini Sián FKP 37, unveiled in 2019, represents Lamborghini's bold step into the hybrid hypercar arena. As the brand’s first hybrid vehicle, the Sián combines traditional V12 power with cutting-edge electric technology, setting new standards for performance and innovation.

At the heart of the Sián is a 6.5-liter V12 engine, producing an astonishing 774 horsepower. This power is complemented by a 48-volt electric motor, adding an additional 34 horsepower. Together, they enable the Sián to accelerate from 0 to 60 mph (0 to 100 km/h) in just 2.8 seconds. The hybrid system also features a supercapacitor, which provides instant power delivery and enhances the car's performance.

The Sián’s design is a fusion of Lamborghini’s iconic styling with modern aerodynamic enhancements. The interior is equally impressive, featuring luxurious materials and advanced technology to deliver an exceptional driving experience.
