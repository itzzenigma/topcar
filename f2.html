Ferrari 812 Competizione: The Pinnacle of V12 Performance

The Ferrari 812 Competizione, unveiled in 2021, stands as a testament to Ferrari's engineering prowess and commitment to high-performance driving. As the ultimate evolution of the 812 Superfast, the 812 Competizione takes V12 performance to new heights with advanced technology and an exhilarating driving experience.

Under the hood of the 812 Competizione is a naturally aspirated 6.5-liter V12 engine that delivers a remarkable 819 horsepower. Paired with a seven-speed dual-clutch transmission, the 812 Competizione accelerates from 0 to 60 mph (0 to 100 km/h) in just 2.8 seconds. The car's advanced aerodynamics, refined chassis, and precise handling ensure an exceptional driving experience both on the road and track.

The interior of the 812 Competizione is designed to reflect its performance-oriented nature, featuring high-quality materials, sport seats, and a driver-focused cockpit. The cabin blends luxury with functionality, providing an immersive experience that highlights Ferrari's attention to detail.
