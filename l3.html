Lamborghini Huracán STO: A Track-Bred Supercar

The Lamborghini Huracán STO, introduced in 2020, represents the ultimate in track-focused performance within the Huracán lineup. Designed with a clear motorsport pedigree, the Huracán STO (Super Trofeo Omologata) brings the thrilling experience of racing to the road.

At the heart of the Huracán STO is a naturally aspirated 5.2-liter V10 engine, producing a staggering 631 horsepower. This power is managed through a rear-wheel-drive system and a seven-speed dual-clutch transmission, allowing the car to accelerate from 0 to 60 mph (0 to 100 km/h) in just 3.0 seconds. The STO also features advanced aerodynamics, a lightweight carbon fiber structure, and enhanced suspension systems for unparalleled track performance.

The interior of the Huracán STO is designed to echo its racing intentions, featuring lightweight materials and a focus on driver engagement. The cabin includes racing-inspired seats, minimalistic controls, and advanced technology, ensuring that the driving experience is both exhilarating and immersive.
