Porsche 930 Turbo: The Turbocharged Icon

The Porsche 930 Turbo, introduced in 1975, is a legendary model known for its turbocharged performance and distinctive design. As the first production Porsche to feature a turbocharged engine, the 930 Turbo set new standards in automotive performance.

With its powerful 3.0-liter turbocharged flat-six engine, the 930 Turbo offered exhilarating acceleration and a thrilling driving experience. Its wide body, distinctive “whale tail” rear spoiler, and aggressive stance made it a standout on the road and track.

The 930 Turbo remains a symbol of Porsche’s engineering prowess and a celebrated icon in the world of performance cars.
