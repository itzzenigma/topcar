Ferrari Roma: The New Face of Ferrari Luxury

The Ferrari Roma, introduced in 2020, represents a modern take on Ferrari's grand touring tradition. Combining elegance with performance, the Roma offers a blend of sophisticated design and cutting-edge technology, making it a standout in the Ferrari lineup.

At the heart of the Roma is a 3.9-liter twin-turbo V8 engine, producing 612 horsepower. This power is managed through an 8-speed dual-clutch transmission, enabling the Roma to accelerate from 0 to 60 mph (0 to 100 km/h) in just 3.4 seconds. The Roma's advanced chassis and dynamic driving modes ensure an engaging and refined driving experience, whether on the open road or city streets.

The interior of the Ferrari Roma is designed to offer a luxurious and driver-focused experience. Featuring high-quality materials, advanced technology, and a spacious cabin, the Roma provides a comfortable yet exhilarating driving environment. The cabin's modern design and technological features enhance the driving experience, making it both enjoyable and practical.
