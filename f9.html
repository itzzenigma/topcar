Ferrari F50: The Race-Bred Supercar

The Ferrari F50, produced from 1995 to 1997, is a supercar that blends Formula 1 technology with road-going performance. Celebrating Ferrari's 50th anniversary, it features a design that emphasizes racing heritage and cutting-edge technology, making it one of the most revered models in Ferrari's history.

Under the hood, the F50 boasts a 4.7-liter V12 engine that produces 513 horsepower, propelling the car from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.7 seconds and reaching a top speed of over 202 mph. Its lightweight carbon fiber construction and advanced aerodynamics ensure exceptional handling and driving dynamics.

The F50's design is characterized by its aggressive styling, including a prominent rear wing and wide stance. Inside, the cabin features a driver-focused layout with minimalistic controls and racing-inspired elements, reflecting its Formula 1 roots.
