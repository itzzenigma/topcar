Ferrari SF90 Stradale: The Pinnacle of Ferrari Engineering

The Ferrari SF90 Stradale, introduced in 2019, represents the apex of Ferrari's technological innovation and performance. As Ferrari's first plug-in hybrid supercar, the SF90 Stradale blends extraordinary power with groundbreaking hybrid technology, setting a new standard in the world of high-performance vehicles.

At the heart of the SF90 Stradale is a twin-turbocharged 4.0-liter V8 engine combined with three electric motors, producing a combined output of 986 horsepower. This power is channeled through an eight-speed dual-clutch transmission, allowing the SF90 Stradale to accelerate from 0 to 60 mph (0 to 100 km/h) in just 2.5 seconds. The car's advanced aerodynamics, sophisticated suspension system, and hybrid powertrain ensure an unparalleled driving experience on both road and track.

The interior of the SF90 Stradale is designed with a focus on both luxury and technology. The cabin features high-quality materials, an advanced infotainment system, and a driver-centric layout, providing an immersive driving experience that reflects Ferrari's commitment to excellence.
