Koenigsegg Gemera: The Ultimate Hyper-GT

The Koenigsegg Gemera, introduced in 2021, represents a groundbreaking leap in hypercar design by combining ultra-high performance with the practicality of a four-seat grand tourer. As Koenigsegg's first four-seater, the Gemera merges luxury, speed, and innovative technology in a way that's unprecedented in the hypercar world.

Under its sleek exterior lies a hybrid powertrain consisting of a 2.0-liter twin-turbocharged three-cylinder engine paired with three electric motors. This setup produces a combined output of 1,700 horsepower and 2,581 Nm of torque, enabling the Gemera to accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in just 1.9 seconds.

The Gemera is designed to offer a comfortable and spacious interior with premium materials and state-of-the-art technology, making it a versatile option for both everyday driving and extraordinary performance. Its advanced hybrid system ensures remarkable efficiency while delivering exceptional power and range.
