We don't really need to explain Koenigsegg to you, do we?

Koenigsegg is a Swedish automotive manufacturer renowned for producing some of the world's fastest, most powerful, and technologically advanced hypercars. Founded in 1994 by Christian von Koenigsegg, the company is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of automotive engineering and design.
