Lamborghini Huracán Sterrato: The Ultimate Off-Road Supercar

The Lamborghini Huracán Sterrato, introduced in 2022, represents a thrilling fusion of supercar performance and off-road capability. Designed to tackle challenging terrains while delivering the exhilarating driving experience expected from a Lamborghini, the Huracán Sterrato pushes the boundaries of what a supercar can achieve.

At the core of the Sterrato is a 5.2-liter V10 engine, producing 602 horsepower. This power is managed through an all-wheel-drive system and a seven-speed dual-clutch transmission, ensuring exceptional performance both on and off the road. The Sterrato features a rugged exterior, increased ground clearance, and advanced suspension systems tailored for off-road adventures.

The interior of the Huracán Sterrato blends high-performance features with off-road practicality. The cabin includes durable materials and essential off-road controls, maintaining the luxurious feel while accommodating the demands of off-road driving.
