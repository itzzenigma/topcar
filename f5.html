Ferrari Portofino M: The Elegant Gran Turismo

The Ferrari Portofino M, unveiled in 2020, represents a refined evolution of Ferrari's entry-level grand tourer. As an upgrade from the original Portofino, the M variant brings enhanced performance, advanced technology, and an even greater focus on luxury and driving pleasure.

Powering the Portofino M is a 3.9-liter twin-turbo V8 engine that delivers an impressive 612 horsepower. This power is managed through an eight-speed dual-clutch transmission, allowing the car to accelerate from 0 to 60 mph (0 to 100 km/h) in just 3.4 seconds. The Portofino M also features updated aerodynamics, a more responsive suspension system, and advanced driver assistance technologies for a superior driving experience.

The interior of the Portofino M combines Ferrari's signature luxury with cutting-edge technology. The cabin is crafted from high-quality materials, with a focus on comfort and elegance. The car features advanced infotainment systems, customizable interior options, and a driver-centric layout that enhances the driving experience.
