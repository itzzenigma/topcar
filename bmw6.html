BMW 2002 Turbo: A Classic Icon

The BMW 2002 Turbo, produced between 1973 and 1975, is a legendary model that marked BMW's foray into high-performance compact cars. Renowned for its innovative turbocharged engine and sporty handling, the 2002 Turbo is a milestone in BMW's history, combining classic design with cutting-edge technology of its time.

Under the hood, the 2002 Turbo features a 2.0-liter inline-four engine with a turbocharger, producing around 170 horsepower. This setup allows the car to deliver impressive acceleration and a thrilling driving experience. The lightweight construction and precise handling further enhance its sporty character, making it a beloved classic among enthusiasts.

The design of the 2002 Turbo is iconic, with its distinct Turbo badging, aggressive front bumper, and wide stance. Inside, the car maintains a driver-focused approach with minimalistic yet functional controls, reflecting its performance-oriented nature.
