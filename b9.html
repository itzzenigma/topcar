Bugatti Centodieci: A Tribute to Excellence

The Bugatti Centodieci, unveiled in 2019, is a remarkable hypercar that pays homage to the legendary Bugatti EB110. With a design that echoes the classic lines of the EB110 and modern engineering prowess, the Centodieci stands as a testament to Bugatti's commitment to blending heritage with innovation. Under the hood, the Centodieci features a powerful W16 engine, delivering exceptional performance and acceleration.

Beyond its striking design, the Centodieci offers an exhilarating driving experience with advanced aerodynamics and cutting-edge technology. Its interior, while maintaining a classic aesthetic, is equipped with the latest in luxury and performance features, ensuring that every journey is both thrilling and comfortable.

With a limited production run, the Centodieci is a rare gem in the automotive world, combining the essence of Bugatti's storied past with the demands of modern high-performance driving.
